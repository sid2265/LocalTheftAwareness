Passenger Safety: Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Keep your belongings close and always in sight.
  • Report suspicious activity or persons to railway staff immediately.
  • Use secure bags and lock zippers when possible.
  • Stay alert, especially during crowded times and at stations.
  • Save emergency and helpline numbers on your phone.

Don'ts

  • Leave bags or valuables unattended.
  • Share travel plans or personal information with strangers.
  • Display expensive items (phones, jewelry) openly.
  • Confront thieves directly; seek help from authorities.
